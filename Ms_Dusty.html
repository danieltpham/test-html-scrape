Avengers: Endgame Review

Ms_Dusty

Date: 26/07/2019

A well made film that is marked as the ending for the MCUThe film was exciting and funny, not as well written as Infinity War but easily a top ten superhero movie. I would say character interaction with each other is the best part of this film. If you like superhero or just adventure movies I would recommend this to you.
