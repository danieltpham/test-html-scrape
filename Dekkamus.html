Avengers: Endgame Review

Dekkamus

Date: 15/06/2019

A great finale to an amazing series, however, due to several plot holes, and obvious ways the could’ve defeated Thanos, too many things seemed unrealistic, the action was great, but was yet another disposable CGI army, Thanos seemed unrealistically more powerful, Iron Man’s death seemed avoidable, they had the time stone and somehow they couldn’t undo Iron Man’s death? Boring at times, too long, it faield to get me interested in the next movies (besides Far From Home) but a solid conclusion.
