Avengers: Endgame Review

RustyDiamonds

Date: 15/01/2021

A great ending to a long-running cinematic storyline. The acting was great, and the movie's length worked to its advantage to set scenes before diving into main plot points. Robert Downey Jr.'s performance was stellar especially, and Tom Holland was especially enjoyable during the final, chaotic fight scene. It is not a perfect movie, but a great watch with a tear-jerking ending, a great end to Tony Stark's character arc.
