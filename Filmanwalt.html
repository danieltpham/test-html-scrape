Avengers: Endgame Review

Filmanwalt

Date: 17/12/2020

....a masterpiece. What was created here with phase 1-3 is simply film history (Spiderman Far from Home then brings phase 3 to a final conclusion). With part 1 and 2 (Infinity War and Endgame) the crown of the Marvel films is secured and I can only be grateful for this visual journey.Endgame in particular is a rollercoaster of emotions and brilliantly staged. Exactly the right film for fans and everyone else will get their entertainment costs, because boredom is not to be found in these 3 hours.All in all, a snap of the fingers of perfection.
